How the testing works

Three moves, same order every time. No mockups, no re-staging, no cherry-picked stills.

Pick a promise

One claim a tool makes about your real work becomes the whole episode. Vague marketing turns into a question that can pass or fail.

Test it on camera

Every tool in a test gets the same real tasks, recorded start to finish. What you see is the actual session, including the parts that break.

Publish the verdict

The episode ends with a plain answer: it works, it does not, or it works with caveats. Failed tools stay failed on the record.

Where the money comes from
