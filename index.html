How testing works

The same three moves, in the same order, every episode.

Pick a promise

One claim a tool makes about real work becomes the whole episode. Marketing language turns into a question that can pass or fail.

Test it on camera

Every tool gets the same task, recorded start to finish. What you see is the real session, including the parts that break.

Publish the verdict

The episode ends with a plain answer: it works, it does not, or it works with caveats. Failed tools stay failed on the record.

Where the money comes from

Some links on this site may become affiliate redirects. If you buy a tool through one, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. That never changes a verdict. Tools are recommended only when they pass, and failed tools stay failed no matter who pays.
